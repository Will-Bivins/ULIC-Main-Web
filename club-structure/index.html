About the Club

The University of Louisville Investment Club (ULIC) is a student run, organized, and managed RSO residing in the College of Business. At it’s core, the ULIC is a space for students and young adults to collaborate on market research and strengthen their understanding of the stock market. We believe that through collaboration and the exchange of ideas, students are able to gain independence through knowledge and prepare themselves for personal financial planning, or find new concepts to use in their careers in investment planning.

Furthermore, the ULIC also hosts various guest speakers to share information about their professional field with club members. This allows members to gain better knowledge and scope on financial professions. The University of Louisville Investment Club's primary mission is to create an environment where students can learn about the stock market and investment planning through hands-on research and financial analysis. As part of its publication process, the club produces weekly financial reports on various market sectors. This research helps students develop analytical skills while learning valuable organization and collaboration techniques.

Publication Process

The goal of the University of Louisville Invesment Club is to provide meaningful and insightful information to it's members and the student body as a whole. It is meant to provide a weekly release that provides a firm level of insight to its readers and club members.

The Club releases weekly sector reports. These reports target a specific area of the market and are each written by one of our sector committees.
